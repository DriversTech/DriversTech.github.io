Driver’s Tips

  • Microsoft doesn’t need you to call them.
  • Don’t click pop-ups or “urgent” ads.
  • Restart Wifi by unplugging for 30 seconds.
  • Save your work often (Ctrl+S).
  • If frozen: Ctrl + Alt + Delete.
  • Reboot before calling.
  • Back up monthly.
